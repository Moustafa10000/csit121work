Lab 06 - Website Ebaluation

Website: https://www.noaa.gov

Usability

  • The menu is easy to find and simple to use.
  • Headings and content are well organized.
  • The site looks good on bothe big and small screens.
  • Text and buttons are clear and easy to read

Accessibilty check

Tool: AccessibiltyChecker.org

  • Audit score: 77%
  • Critical Issues: 10
  • Passed Audits: 42
  • Manual Audits: 3

Most issues are from low color contrast. this can make text hard to read for people with low vision or color blindess

Recommendations

  • Make text and background colors higher contrast
  • Use more descriptive link text instead of "Read more".
  • Keep the menu easy to find on all screen sizes.
